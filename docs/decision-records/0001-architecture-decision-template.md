# ADR-0001 — Architecture Decision Record template

- **Status:** accepted (this is the template all ADRs follow)
- **Date:** 2026-06-24

> Architecture Decision Records capture *consequential* choices and the reasoning
> behind them, so future readers understand not just what was decided but why, and
> what was given up. One ADR per decision. ADRs are immutable once accepted; to
> revisit one, write a new ADR that supersedes it.

Copy the structure below for new records (`NNNN-short-title.md`, zero-padded,
incrementing).

---

## Context

What is the situation, constraint, or force that requires a decision? Reference the
relevant law(s) in [SOUL.md](../SOUL.md) where applicable — The Familiar is telos-first,
so most consequential decisions trace to the Three Laws.

## Decision

What was decided, stated plainly.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what is given up. Include the honest
downsides — an ADR that only lists benefits is incomplete.

## Alternatives considered

The serious options not taken, and why.
